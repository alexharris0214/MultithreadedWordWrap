Pneumonoultramicroscopicsilicovolcanoconiosis
is a word coined by the president of the
National Puzzlers' League as a synonym
for the disease known as silicosis. It
is the longest word in the English
language published in a dictionary,
Oxford Dictionaries, which defines it as
"an artificial long word said to mean a
lung disease caused by inhaling very
fine ash and sand dust".
